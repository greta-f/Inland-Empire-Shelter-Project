County Shelter Statistics

Key Insights

This dashboard presents county-level animal shelter statistics, including intake, outcomes, and capacity trends across the Inland Empire. The data highlights increasing intake pressures and variability in outcomes across jurisdictions.

Patterns in the data suggest that shelters are experiencing sustained strain, with intake often outpacing adoption and transfer rates. These trends help explain the overcrowding conditions observed in many facilities.
